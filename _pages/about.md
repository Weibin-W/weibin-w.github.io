Weibin Wu is currently an Associate Professor of School of Software Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University. His research interests include trustworthy artificial intelligence, large language model, computer vision, and intelligent software engineering. Professor Wu holds a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Automation from Tongji University in 2017 and obtained his Doctor of Philosophy degree in Computer Science and Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, co-advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu and Prof. Irwin King.
