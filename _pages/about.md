Hello! I am currently an Associate Professor of School of Software Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University. I hold a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Automation from Tongji University in 2017 and obtained my Doctor of Philosophy degree in Computer Science and Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, co-advised by [Prof. Michael R. Lyu](https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/lyu) and [Prof. Irwin King](https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/irwin.king).

My research interests include trustworthy artificial intelligence, large language model, computer vision, and intelligent software engineering. 


My chinese homepage is at [this link](https://sse.sysu.edu.cn/teacher/249).
